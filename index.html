Publications

Saudi-Dialect-ALLaM: LoRA Fine-Tuning for Dialectal Arabic Generation

arXiv:2508.12345 • 19 Aug 2025

Read

Most Arabic LLMs focus on Modern Standard Arabic, overlooking Saudi dialects like Najdi and Hijazi. We fine-tuned ALLaM-7B with a Saudi Dialect dataset and found that adding dialect tags greatly improved control and text quality. Our tuned models outperformed leading instruction models, and we share code and evaluation tools for reproducibility.
